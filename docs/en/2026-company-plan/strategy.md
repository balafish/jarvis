# 2026 Strategic Planning

## Vision

> "Become a small but mighty R&D company in Vietnam, aligned with world-leading technical capabilities, pioneering AGI applications"

## Mission

As an AI-driven lean team, provide high-quality software R&D services to the group, develop revenue-generating products, and achieve break-even by 2027 Q2.

---

## Core Strategies

### Strategy 1: AI-First Development Model

**Goal:** AI as core productivity, humans as AI collaborators

**Action Plans:**
1. Fully implement AI development tools (Vibe Coding, Cursor, Copilot)
2. Establish AI-assisted workflows
3. Cultivate team AI application capabilities

**Expected Outcome:** Significant increase in per-capita output, achieve more with less

---

### Strategy 2: Product Monetization

**Goal:** Transform from cost center to profit center

**Action Plans:**
1. Optimize anti-hijacking product, develop new features
2. Negotiate commission model with Siraya (5-10%)
3. Target 50 customers

**Expected Outcome:** Establish stable product revenue stream

---

### Strategy 3: Lean Team Building

**Goal:** 15-person team with complete R&D capabilities

**Action Plans:**
1. Q1 hire Technical Lead (entrepreneurial mindset)
2. Expand to 15 people on schedule
3. Establish SOP and project management systems

**Expected Outcome:** Independent and robust R&D company

---

### Strategy 4: International Integration

**Goal:** Implement cross-border collaboration, integrate group resources

**Action Plans:**
1. Strengthen collaboration with group companies (Siraya, Sky-Net, Tomo)
2. Increase personnel exchange frequency
3. Participate in cross-border projects

**Expected Outcome:** Enhanced international vision and capabilities

---

## SWOT Analysis

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Local Vietnam company, cost advantage | Small team size |
| International group resource support | Currently loss-making |
| Product already launched (Anti-hijacking) | Single revenue source |
| Leading in AI application | Lack of technical lead |

| Opportunities | Threats |
|---------------|---------|
| Vietnam as key foreign investment destination | Talent competition |
| AGI application market growth | Exchange rate fluctuation |
| Internal group project demand | Customer dependency on Siraya |
| Anti-hijacking product market demand | Product competition |

### Differentiated Positioning

- **Small but mighty:** AI-enhanced to achieve large team output
- **AGI Pioneer:** Leading application of latest AI technology
- **Group Resources:** Backed by international group, not fighting alone

### Core Competencies

1. AI-native development capability
2. Local Vietnam + international vision
3. Group product and resource integration
