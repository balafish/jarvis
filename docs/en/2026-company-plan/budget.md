## Revenue Projections

### Scenario 1: Service Fee + Maintenance Fee (Conservative)

- Siraya service fee (ongoing)
- Product maintenance fee (fixed monthly)
- Estimated annual revenue: **Below spending limit**

### Scenario 2: Service Fee + Commission Model (50 Customers)

- Siraya service fee (ongoing)
- Anti-hijacking product commission (per contract terms)
- Estimated annual revenue: **Near or above spending limit**

**Note:** Commission model has higher revenue potential and is key to achieving break-even.

## Profit/Loss Projections

| Scenario | Expected Result |
|----------|-----------------|
| Scenario 1 (Conservative) | Loss, requires investment subsidy |
| Scenario 2 (Commission) | Near break-even or slight profit |

**Conclusion:** To achieve break-even by 2027 Q2, we need to push the commission model and achieve customer targets.

## Budget Control Mechanisms

1. Monthly financial report review
2. Quarterly budget allocation adjustments
